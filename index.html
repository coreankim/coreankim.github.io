About Me

I'm an east coast transplant trying to survive in an orthopaedic surgery residency program in Cleveland! I'm also learning how to code because I think it will be a very important skillset.


My hobbies include trying to get fit, eating good food, and having fun! I hope that by the end of this class, I will have the skills to develop a few apps to increase the efficiency of residents doing their job. I'll start with doable applications, such as scheduling apps tailored to my residency program, and then move on to apps that help with the clinical diagnosis of emergent conditions such as septic arthritis, traumatic arthrotomy, and compartment syndrome!
